# User Stories — Predator Identification Network (PIN)

## Poacher (independent)
- As an independent poacher, I want to register and complete role verification so my submissions carry credibility.
- As an independent poacher, I want to submit a new predator record with supporting evidence and have the AI cross-confirmation gate evaluate it before it goes public.
- As an independent poacher, I want to see why a submission was rejected so I can address gaps and resubmit.
- As an independent poacher, I want to publish documents and videos and choose whether each item is free or premium.
- As an independent poacher, I want to opt my premium content into the sitewide subscription pool so I earn from a wider audience.
- As an independent poacher, I want to see my monthly payout breakdown with the exact formula inputs so I trust the distribution.
- As an independent poacher, I want a public profile page that shows my listed predators and content output.

## Poacher (group member)
- As a group lead investigator, I want to create a group, set the revenue split among members, and invite contributors.
- As a group member, I want a shared workspace with a document vault and case workbench so we can collaborate without sending files in DMs.
- As a group member, I want to publish content under the group identity and have revenue split per our agreement.
- As a group lead, I want to see the group's stats (predators listed, status counts, subscriber count) on the public group explore page.

## Expert / journalist / legal representative
- As an expert, I want to verify my credentials once and then carry weighted review authority on submissions inside my domain.
- As a legal representative, I want to mark a predator's status transition (e.g. `CURRENTLY_PROSECUTED` → `POST_CONVICTION`) when I have authoritative knowledge of a case outcome.
- As a journalist, I want to corroborate a submission with a published article and have my corroboration count toward the AI gate's "independent source" requirement.

## General user / subscriber
- As a general user, I want to browse the predator explore page and filter by status, jurisdiction, and date listed.
- As a general user, I want to view a predator profile page with their status timeline and evidence so I can verify a concern.
- As a general user, I want to subscribe-for-updates on a specific predator so I get notified when their status changes.
- As a subscriber, I want to subscribe to a single poacher I respect and unlock their premium content.
- As a subscriber, I want a sitewide subscription that unlocks every participating poacher's premium content so I don't have to manage many subscriptions.
- As a subscriber, I want to comment on predator profile pages with my comments subject to moderation.

## Content creator
- As a content creator (non-submission role), I want to upload content tied to existing predator records or to general topics, and monetize free/premium tiers.

## Politician
- As a politician, I want a verified-badge presence on the platform that shows my support without granting me submission authority.

## Subject of listing (mis-listing dispute)
- As someone who has been listed and believes I was wrongly listed, I want to file a verified-identity dispute and have it reviewed.
- As a disputant, I want a clear path to the external review board if the platform's first decision goes against me.
- As a disputant, I want my dispute to be confidential during review.

## Platform reviewer
- As a platform reviewer, I want a queue of `needs_human_review` submissions sorted by AI confidence so I can triage efficiently.
- As a platform reviewer, I want to see the full AI pipeline output (fetched sources, citations, confidence) for each submission so I can validate the AI's decision.
- As a platform reviewer, I want to issue strikes for clearly bad-faith submissions and see the submitter's strike history.

## DMCA / takedown requester
- As a DMCA claimant, I want a clear takedown form and a predictable response window.
- As a counter-notice filer, I want a clear counter-notice path that puts the burden of proof back on the original claimant.

## Anti-personas (people the platform must NOT serve well)
- The doxxer who wants to add a personal-enemy to the registry with thin evidence — the AI gate must reject this submission.
- The vigilante who wants to coordinate real-world action — v1 has no functionality enabling this; phase-2 only with formal law-enforcement liaison framework.
- The brigading bad-actor who wants to drive comment swarms against a listed person — moderation queues and rate limits apply.
