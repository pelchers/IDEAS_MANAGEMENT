Prisma Models

Subscription

  • userId, stripeCustomerId
  • stripeSubscriptionId (unique)
  • status: ACTIVE | PAST_DUE | CANCELED | TRIALING | UNPAID
  • plan: FREE | PRO | TEAM
  • currentPeriodStart, currentPeriodEnd
  • cancelAtPeriodEnd

Entitlement

  • userId + feature (unique together)
  • grantedAt, expiresAt (optional)
  • source: SUBSCRIPTION | ADMIN_GRANT | TRIAL

BillingEvent

  • stripeEventId (unique for idempotency)
  • eventType, stripeCustomerId
  • data (Json), processedAt
