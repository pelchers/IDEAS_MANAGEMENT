Studio Companion

Exchange letters with your AI design assistant

AL
Astrid Lindqvist 9:15 AM

I am looking for inspiration for our Spring Meadow collection photoshoot. We want warm, natural light with a lived-in Scandinavian feel. What locations or setups would you suggest?

Studio Companion 9:16 AM

Lovely direction, Astrid. For the Spring Meadow collection, I would suggest three complementary setups:

1. The Sunlit Studio -- A whitewashed room with south-facing windows. Place pieces on raw oak floors with linen drapes catching morning light. Add a few dried wildflower stems in a ceramic vase.

2. The Garden Room -- A conservatory or greenhouse setting where natural greenery frames the furniture. Moss and ferns provide organic contrast to the blush upholstery.

3. The Cottage Interior -- A real Danish summer cottage with whitewashed walls, worn wooden beams, and that authentic sense of hygge. Layer the textiles over vintage chairs beside a woodstove.

Here is a shot list for the Blomst Armchair:
