AI Chat

Brainstorm with your AI assistant

AI

Hey there! I'm your creative brainstorming assistant. Tell me about your project and I'll help you explore ideas, suggest improvements, or think through problems.

I need ideas for improving user onboarding in our app. The current flow has a 40% drop-off rate.

JD
AI

Great question! Here are some approaches to reduce onboarding drop-off:

  • Progressive disclosure - Show only essential steps first, reveal advanced features later
  • Interactive tutorial - Let users complete real tasks during onboarding instead of passive walkthroughs
  • Personalization - Ask what they want to achieve and tailor the first experience
  • Quick wins - Help them create their first idea or project within 60 seconds

Which of these resonates most with your user base?

The progressive disclosure approach sounds great. Can you sketch out what the first 3 steps might look like?

JD
AI

Here's a 3-step progressive onboarding flow:

Step 1 - Welcome & Intent (15 sec): Single screen asking "What brings you here?" with 3 big illustrated cards: "Brainstorm Ideas", "Manage Projects", "Collaborate with Team"

Step 2 - First Creation (30 sec): Based on their choice, guide them to create their first item. Pre-filled templates make it feel effortless.

Step 3 - Quick Tour (45 sec): Highlight 3 key areas of the app relevant to their choice. Use tooltips, not modals.
